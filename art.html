What is art?

Art is a medium of expression that requires creativity and imagination.

It has the capability to reflect beauty of anything through various methods.

Art helps an individual to develop the creative side of his life.

About Artsy

Artsy is a website that created for anyone who is interested in painting.

Our aim is to inspire you to create art.

This website was created with the intention encouraging aspiring artists.
